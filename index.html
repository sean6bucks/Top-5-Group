TOP 5 asked artists from different disciplines to each create a series of 5 posters using only 5 colors reimagining their TOP 5 biggest artistic influences from movies, TV, books, comics, music, etc.

TOP 5 请了来自不同领域的艺术家创作并提交5组作品，主题是“最影响你的5个电影、电视、书籍、漫画、音乐等流行艺术形式”。每一个参展人作品只限于5个颜色（可以运用同种颜色的不同色阶）。

Beryl Chung and the crew who brought you Happy Birthday Elvis Fest present TOP 5, a Shanghai community art event celebrating pop culture.
